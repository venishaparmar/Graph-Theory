Cycle Graph

A cycle graph is a graph that consists of a single cycle, or in other words, some number of vertices connected in a closed chain.

The cycle graph with n vertices is called Cn.

Examples of Cycle Graph

Cycle Graph C4 with 4 vertices

1 2 3 4 1

Cycle Graph C5 with 5 vertices

1 2 3 4 5 1

Cycle Graph C6 with 6 vertices

1 2 3 4 5 6 1

Cycle Graph C7 with 7 vertices
